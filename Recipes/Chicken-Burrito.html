Chicken Burrito

Prep: 15 min
Cook: 25 min
Serves: 3-4

Ingredients

Chicken Filling:

  • 500g boneless chicken breast, diced
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

To Assemble:

  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup canned black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Fresh cilantro and lime wedges

Instructions

  1. Mix cumin, paprika, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper together.
  2. Toss diced chicken in the spice mix until evenly coated.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook chicken for 6-8 minutes until cooked through and slightly charred. Set aside.
  4. Warm black beans and corn in the same skillet for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Warm tortillas in a dry pan or microwave for 20 seconds until pliable.
  6. Lay a tortilla flat and layer rice in the center, followed by beans, corn, and chicken.
  7. Top with cheese, salsa, sour cream, and avocado slices.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro and a squeeze of lime.
  9. Fold in the sides of the tortilla, then roll tightly from the bottom up.
  10. Optional: toast the wrapped burrito in a pan seam-side down for 1-2 minutes for a crispy exterior.

Chef's Tips

  • Don't overfill the tortilla or it will be hard to roll
  • Use warmed tortillas to prevent tearing while rolling
  • Marinate chicken for 30 minutes for deeper flavor
  • Add pickled jalapeños for extra heat
  • Wrap tightly in foil to hold shape until serving
