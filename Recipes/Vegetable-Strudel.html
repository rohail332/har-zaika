Vegetable Strudel

Prep: 20 min
Cook: 30 min
Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 6 sheets phyllo pastry
  • 3 tbsp olive oil or melted butter
  • 1 cup spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 zucchini, grated and squeezed dry
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (390°F). Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan over medium heat. Sauté garlic, bell pepper, and mushrooms for 5 minutes.
  3. Add spinach and zucchini, cook for 2 more minutes until wilted. Season with thyme, salt, and pepper.
  4. Remove from heat, let cool slightly, then mix in feta cheese.
  5. Lay one phyllo sheet on a clean surface, brush lightly with oil. Layer all 6 sheets, brushing each with oil.
  6. Spread the vegetable filling along one long edge, leaving a 2 cm border.
  7. Roll up tightly like a log and place seam-side down on the baking tray.
  8. Brush the top with remaining oil and score lightly with a knife.
  9. Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden and crispy.
  10. Let rest for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.

Chef's Tips

  • Keep phyllo sheets covered with a damp cloth to prevent drying out
  • Squeeze all moisture from zucchini to avoid a soggy strudel
  • Add sun-dried tomatoes for extra depth of flavor
  • Serve with a side of tzatziki or sour cream
