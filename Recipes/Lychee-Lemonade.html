Lychee Lemonade

Prep: 10 min
Cook: 0 min
Serves: 2-3

Ingredients

  • 1 can (20 oz) lychees in syrup
  • 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice (about 4 lemons)
  • 3 cups cold water
  • 2 tbsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • Ice cubes
  • Lemon slices and mint for garnish

Instructions

  1. Drain lychees, reserving the syrup.
  2. Blend lychees until smooth, then strain through a fine mesh sieve.
  3. In a pitcher, combine lychee puree, reserved syrup, lemon juice, and water.
  4. Add sugar and stir until dissolved. Taste and adjust sweetness.
  5. Fill glasses with ice cubes and pour in the lemonade.
  6. Garnish with lemon slices and fresh mint. Serve immediately.

Chef's Tips

  • Use fresh lychees when in season for a more intense flavor
  • Chill the pitcher for 30 minutes before serving for best taste
  • Add a splash of sparkling water for a fizzy version
  • Rose water (1 tsp) pairs beautifully with lychee
